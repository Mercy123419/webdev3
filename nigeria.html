Nigeria

Nigeria is a vibrant and culturally rich country located in West Africa. It is the most populous country in Africa and known for its diverse ethnic groups, beautiful landscapes, and thriving music and film industries. The country plays a key role in African economics and politics. Nigeria has 36 states. The capital of Nigeria is ABUJA.
