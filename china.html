China

China, located in East Asia, is one of the world's oldest civilizations and currently the most populous country on Earth. Known for its historical landmarks like the Great Wall and the Forbidden City, China boasts rich traditions, modern megacities, and a strong global economy. It plays a major role in global culture, technology, and trade. The capital of China is Beijing.
