About CountryFacts

CountryFacts is your go-to website for learning fascinating details about countries all around the world. Whether you're a student researching for school, a traveler seeking insights, or just someone who loves geography, this site has something for you!

You'll find fun facts, population data, languages, and short histories of countries from every continent— all neatly organized and easy to navigate.

This project was created by Mercy Abimaje to educate and inspire others through simple, clean design and accurate information.

Explore, learn, and be amazed by the diversity of our world.
